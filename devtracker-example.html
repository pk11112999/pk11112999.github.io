Welcome to NYC Development Tracker

A citizen's simple tool to know who's building what in the city

This website offers users the ability to search recent New York City building permit application filings. The data are provided by the Department of Buildings through NYC OpenData, and the dataset is updated daily.

What does the map show?

It shows the locations of the buildings

What does the table show?

It shows the location and permit details

Search by selecting the timeframe first, then the borough, followed by community board, and finally click 'Search'
